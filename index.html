How We Met

Join us on a journey through our love story, from the moment we met to the adventures that have made our bond stronger.

A New York Beginning: Our Love Story

2022

I drove up from D.C. to New York City, heart pounding with anticipation to finally meet her. We had only talked before, but seeing her in person at a cozy Manhattan caf√© felt surreal. That evening, we boarded a cruise along the Hudson River. As the sun set, we shared stories and laughter, feeling a connection that grew stronger with each moment. Later, we explored the vibrant streets of Times Square, hand in hand, feeling like we were in our own world despite the bustling crowds. As we stood amidst the city's lights, I realized I was falling for her, knowing this was the beginning of something beautiful.

Tropical Journey: Falling Deeper in Love

2023

In the months that followed, we found ourselves immersed in a whirlwind of shared adventures and quiet moments. We explored new places, but our favorite journey was to Honolulu, Hawaii. There, we wandered along pristine beaches, hand in hand, as the waves serenaded us with their soothing rhythm. We indulged in the vibrant local food, savoring every bite and discovering new flavors together. Immersing ourselves in the island's rich culture, we attended traditional luaus and learned about the history that shaped this paradise. Each day spent together deepened our connection, and as we watched the sun set over the ocean, we knew we had found something truly special in each other.

Back to My Roots: Love Blossoms in the Himalayas

2023

Returning to Nepal after 15 years felt like coming home to a place that had been waiting for me. Exploring the remote cities of Ghandruk and Pokhara with her by my side was both an adventure and a homecoming. Meeting her family and reuniting with my own relatives deepened our connection, as we shared stories and laughter across generations. The warmth and hospitality of our families made every moment special, and the beauty of the Himalayas provided a breathtaking backdrop for our journey. As we immersed ourselves in the rich culture and traditions, I realized how much our lives had become intertwined, and how this trip had strengthened our bond in ways I had never imagined.

Sun, Sand, and Serenity

2024

Our adventures took us to the neon oasis of Las Vegas, where vibrant lights and dazzling shows electrified our senses. But it was in the natural wonders that our hearts truly sparkled. We hiked hand-in-hand through Zion's red rock canyons, marveling at the beauty that surrounded us. And then, we found serenity in the crystal-clear waters and majestic mountains of Lake Tahoe, where breathtaking drives and relaxing beach days left us awestruck. As we explored these stunning destinations together, our love grew stronger, forever bonding us in the beauty and wonder of our adventures.
